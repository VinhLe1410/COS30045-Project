Gender Disparity in Physician Workforce of Australia 2012 - 2021

About the Visualisation

This visualisation provides user with detailed comparison between male and female physicians in Australia from 2012 to 2021. Data for total male and female, as well as data for each age group of a gender, is used for this visualisation.
