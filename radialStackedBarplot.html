Population of Physicians of OECD Members and Partners by Age Groups in 2021.

About the Visualisation

This visualisation aims to compare number of physicians divided into age groups among OECD member and partner countries in 2020. Total, female, and male physicians are used for this comparison.
The chart provided buttons to choose among radial stacked charts for Total (both female and male physicians), and Female, and Male specific physicians. While reading the chart, web users could mouse-over on a bar to show its specific data of number of physicians on the hovered country and age group.
